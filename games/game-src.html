You all awake in your beds to the sound of a siren striking fear into your hearts. In the small Appalachian town of Mystic River you almost never hear sirens. They always bring terrible news.

Today is no exception. You leave your houses, some half-dressed, and head towards the source of the ominous sound. There is a commotion out by the docks and you form a crowd around the police tape to watch the sheriff and his deputy dragging a body from the river that is your town's namesake. Some scream, some burst into tears, others demand to see who it is, to know what happened to him. The officers roll the sodden corpse onto its back, in view of the crowd.

"NO! It's Death Butt!" cries Dr Agon.

"No," replies the sheriff in gruff despair. "It's Dead Butt."

Death Butt, beloved mayor and friend to all residents of Mystic River lies dead before you. His flesh is pale and ghostly, his dripping clothes stained in dark blood mixed with the mud of the river.

"What happened to him?" is the cry from AngryNarwhal, shouting above the hushed whispers, gasps and sobs.

The sheriff stands in front of the body and addresses the crowd seriously, slowly eyeing everyone from behind his dark aviator sunglasses.

"He's been murdered."

Not a single murder has occured in your town for as long as anyone can remember. The sheriff and his deputy spend most of their time assisting the residents of their small jurisdiction and making arrangements in the rare occasion someone passes from disease or accident; crime is hardly even in their job description. Shocked and confused, but mostly deeply saddened, the crowd disperses to resume their days while a few stay behind to assist the policemen with the body.

The next day Xazarus is found dead in the street, bloody and beaten, but nobody seems to care too much. The police scrape him off the pavement.

Several days later, the whole town turns out for Death Butt's funeral. It is a solemn affair and everyone takes a turn to say a few words about their departed friend.

As the burial draws to a close, the sheriff addresses the crowd.

"As you all know, Death Butt was murdered. My deputy and I have been investigating to the best of our ability but have not been able to uncover much. He was found along the riverside by some passing boaters, who called us to the scene. He had recieved several blows to the head and body as well as some minor lacerations. It appears that the cause of death was blunt trauma to the head and that he was killed a few hours after midnight. We are asking you to help us in our search for the killer.

"Each day we must choose one person to be hanged by the neck until dead, until the murders stop. If they are innocent they will join Death Butt at the foot of Our Lord. If they are not, well, I think we all know what will happen to them."
