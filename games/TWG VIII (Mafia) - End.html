Basic Rules
There are 2 teams, Villager(Humans) and the Thugs (Wolves) the object for either team is to eliminate the opposing team. Rounds are divided into nights and days.  During the day, all players deliberate on who they think are Thugs and cast votes to kill.  During the night, the Thugs decide on a target and are allowed to kill someone off. the Villagers win when all the Thugs are dead, and the Thugs win when Thugs >= Villagers.

-No talking at night. (This means no posting in this thread until I say the day has started)
-No private messaging to another player, or outside discussion, meaning all discussion must happen publicly via posting. (Thugs are allowed to communicate to each other privately)

-you may change your vote twice per 'day' (Total, 3).
-Thugs and special roles must send their nighttime kills/guards/sights within a reasonable period of time, preferably less than 24 hours.

-No posting after you are dead.
