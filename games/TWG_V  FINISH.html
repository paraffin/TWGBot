Dinosaurs vs. Time-Traveling Dragons

Dragons got a hold of time traveling technology and traveled back in time for some reason. They brought dark materia and are attempting to summon a meteor to the planet in an attempt to kill all the dinosaurs. Your mission: you must find the dragons hiding amongst your dinosaur comrades and eliminate them.

Basic Rules:
There are 2 teams, Time-Traveling Dragons(wolves) and Dinosaurs(humans).  the object for either team is to eliminate the opposing team. Rounds are divided into nights and days.  During the day, all players deliberate on who they think are Dragons and cast votes to kill.  During the night, the Dragons decide on a target and are allowed to kill someone off. the Dinosaurs win when all the Dragons are dead, and the Dragons win when Dragons >= Dinosaurs.

Other Rules:
-No talking at night (This means no posting in this thread until I say the day has started)
-No private messaging to another player, or outside discussion, meaning all discussion must happen publicly via posting. (Dragons are allowed to communicate to each other privately)
-you must post at least once per 'day', or you will automatically get a vote for yourself
-you may only vote once per 'day', meaning votes are permanent and cannot be changed once cast.
-the day will automatically end if one player receives a majority of votes
-Dragons and special roles must send their nighttime kills/guards/sights within a reasonable period of time, preferably less than 24 hours
-you may not copy/post any private messages from myself or other players into the game
-No posting if you are dead(absolutely no outside discusion of the game for reasons that may or may not become obvious through course of the game)
-All rules will be subject to my discretion

Guaranteed Special Roles:
Seer- During the night, a seer has the ability to view one player, however the seer only sees alignment ex. if the seer views the Guardian they would see only human(Dinosaur), this is different than the other games in that the seer could tell the exact alignment, role, powers etc.

in general (as a human seer) you want to target people who you think may be a dragon.  If you find some fellow dinosaurs, you can try to form some kind of alliance.  Be careful, because if you tell others you are the seer, you will become a big target for the dragons.

Guardian- During the night, a guardian may choose one player to guard.  This player is protected from the dragons' kill that night. If a player is successfully guarded (meaning it blocks a night kill), I will announce that there was no night kill.  Guardian cannot guard themselves, and cannot guard the same person twice in a row.

in general you want to protect who you believe the wolves are going to kill.  Keep this in mind if the seer comes out.  Also, you can rarely be positive that anyone claiming to be a special is or not.
